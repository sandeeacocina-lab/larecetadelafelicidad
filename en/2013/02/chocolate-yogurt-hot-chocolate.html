Spanish hot chocolate and chocolate yogurt

Summary: Hot chocolate recipe, Spanish style. Chill overnight, and get a creamy chocolate yogurt. Or reheat in the microwave for 30 sec to enjoy a hot chocolate whenever you want!

Preparation time: 5 minute(s) Cooking time: 15 minute(s)

Number of servings (yield): 10

Ingredients

  • 250 g dark chocolate
  • 100 g sugar
  • 40 g cornstarch
  • 1 l milk
  • Optional: cinnamon powder, vanilla extract,..

Directions

  1. Whisk together cornstarch and 1/2 cup milk until smooth.
  2. Cook remaining milk and sugar in a large saucepan over medium heat until it boils.
  3. Whisk in chocolate and stir until smooth, whisking constantly
  4. Whisk in cornstarch mixture, and whisk until mixture thickens (mixture will thicken more as it cools.) Serve immediately with "churros"
  5. Alternatively, pour in yogurt pots, and keep in the fridge for 24 hours. Next day you will get a delicious chocolate "yogurt". If you prefer to enjoy it hot, you just have to reheat it in the microwave for about 45 seconds.
NOTES:
  • If it is your first time preparing this recipe, you should make a test to verify your ideal reheating time in the microwave. According to my experience, it might take from 30 sec to 1 minute to get your chocolate "yogurt" become a delicious hot chocolate. So try initially with 30 seconds, stir, and if necessary add 15 sec more. Stir once more, verify, and if it is not hot enough, add 10-15 more seconds in the microwave
