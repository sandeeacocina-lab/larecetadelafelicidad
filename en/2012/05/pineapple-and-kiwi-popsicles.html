Pineapple and kiwi popsicles

prep time:

5 min

cooking time:

5 min

total:

10 min plus freezing time

yield:

8-10 popsicles

Ingredients : 200 ml water 100 gr sugar 500 ml unsweetened pinneaple juice 2 ripe kiwifruits peeled and sliced

Popsicle molds. I have used

these ones
Preparation: Put water and sugar into a saucepan and boil without stirring for 5 minutes. Let it cool. When cooled, add pineapple juice and mix with a spoon. Place kiwi slices on the popsicle molds. Fill in the molds with juice. Freeze for at least 6 hours, better overnight. Enjoy your homemade pinneaple and kiwifruit popsicles.
