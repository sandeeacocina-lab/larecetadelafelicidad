Strawberry powder and homemade Pixie Sticks

Strawberry powder

Summary: Dehydrated strawberry powder, after this recipe from 1 More Spoon

Ingredients

  • 250 g strawberries

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 150°F.
  2. Wash strawberries and slice them into very thin slices.
  3. Place the strawberry slices onto two parchment lines baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the oven for 2-3 hrs or until dry.
  5. Take the strawberry slices and let them rest 24 hours at room temperature. (the original recipe suggests placing them in the freezer for 10 minutes)
  6. Grind the slices using a food processor. Keep the powder in an airtight container and use at your convenience, for example in these recipes: Decorated cake roll and strawberry cookies

Preparation time: 15 minute(s)

Cooking time: 2 hour(s)

Number of servings (yield): 25 g powdered strawberries







Homemade Pixie Sticks

Summary: Homemade Pixie stiks, inspired by this idea

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dehydrated powdered strawberry or raspberry
  • 1-2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • paper straws (you can buy them here)

Directions

  1. Mix strawberry powder and sugar and pour this mixture into a paper cone
  2. Seal the end of every paper straw using some fondant paste (or melted chocolate-you'll have to let it set- or just by folding the paper straw's end)
  3. Insert every straw into the paper cone to get the strawberries powder and sugar mix in easily.(the original recipe proposes a different way to fill the paper straws, but I found it too difficult, it almost take 10 minutes to fill each stick!)
  4. Seal the other end of the straw, and enjoy like a kid.

Preparation time: 10 minute(s)

Number of servings (yield): 12 Pixie Sticks
