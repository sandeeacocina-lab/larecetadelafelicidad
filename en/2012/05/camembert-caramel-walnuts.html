Camembert, caramel and walnuts kit

Descripción: An edible gift for this Christmas: a box of Camembert , sugar, walnuts and the recipe to prepare a delicious appetizer, after this idea from BBC Good Food

Ingredientes

  • 100 g sugar
  • 50 gr walnuts
  • 1 boxed Camembert
  • To complete the kit: small jar, string or ribbon and a label with the recipe

Preparación

  1. Put the camembert wheel onto a plate or platter.
  2. Sprinkle on top of the cheese with the walnuts.
  3. Put sugar in a heavy-based pan.
  4. Cook on a low heat, without stirring, for 2-3 minutes or until sugar has melted and is dark golden brown.
  5. The pan may need to be tilted to ensure sugar melts evenly.
  6. Remove from heat and pour caramel over cheese and nuts.

Preparation time: 5 minute(s)

Cooking time: 5 minute(s)

Number of servings (yield): 4
