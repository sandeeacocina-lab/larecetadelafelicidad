Summer water

prep time:

5 min

total:

5 min plus freezing time

Ingredients :

500 ml natural fruit juice: I have used apricot, and pinneaple and berries
Ice cube trays
1 liter water

Preparation

:
Pour fruit juice in ice cube trays. Freeze for 24 hours. Put fruit ice cubes until 1/4 of glass, and fill in with water. Serve immediately.
