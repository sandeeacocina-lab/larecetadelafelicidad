Chocolate pop corn

Ingredients:

  • 250 g dark chocolate
  • 1 package microwave corn, popped

Directions:

  1. Melt chocolate in the microwave, or using a double boiler, stirring continously.
  2. Let it cool for a few minutes, add pop corn and gently stir in the chocolate, ensuring that all pop corn is covered with chocolate.
  3. Then spread the chocolate popcorn on a paper parchment, and let it set for a few hours until dry. In summer time it is better to keep the chocolate pop corn in fridge, inside an airtight container.

Preparation time: 15 min

Cooking time: 1,5 min

Yield:4 servings
